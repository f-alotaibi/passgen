What is the purpose of this website?

The main purpose is to make users use randomized passwords/passphrases,
since a lot of people use shared passwords in all their accounts, which could be prone to disastrous consequences.

What is a passphrase?

A passphrase is a bunch of words that is longer than a typical password, but is easier to remember to the person
however, a passphrase that has context is much easier to crack hence generated passphases are the preferable choice.

What is a password entropy?

Password entropy is a measuremeant of how unpredictable a password is and is expressed in terms of bits,
the higher the bit, the harder to crack your password,
you can check your password's entropy in here.
